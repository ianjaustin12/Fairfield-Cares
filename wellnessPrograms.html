Mental Health Programs/Resources

Struggling with isolation, stress, depression, substance use or other issues? Help is here.

All Ages

  • Fairfield Department of Human & Social Services: Information & connections to supports such as food, mental health referrals, financial assistance. 203-256-3169
  • Fairfield Health Department: Education, blood pressure and cholesterol screenings, walk-in clinics, and routine & travel immunizations. 203-256-3020
  • Fairfield Counseling Services: High quality mental health and substance abuse services to adults, youth, and families. 203-255-5777 x 15
  • Fairfield CARES: Designated local prevention council. Subscribe to email list for youth & family resources.

Youth

  • Fairfield PD Youth Bureau:Casework, referrals, mentoring. 203-254-4835
  • Teen Talk: Free counseling provided by Kids in Crisis through 24/7 hotline: 203- 661-1911
  • Fairfield CARES Youth Committee: Teen-led youth arm of Fairfield CARES
  • Wakeman Boys and Girls Club: Provides young people, ages 5 to 18, with safe activities through after-school, weekend, and summer programs. 203-908-3381
  • Town Youth Counsil: HS board led by the Recreation Department's Youth Services Coordinator. 203-256-3191

Older Adults

  • Bigelow Center for Senior Activities: Programs & support. 203-256-3166
  • Senior Services: Transportation 203-256-3168, Handyman Program 203-256-3120

Behavioral Health

  • Positive Directions-The Center for Prevention & Counseling: Mental health & substance use counseling, prevention, support groups. 203-227-7644
  • TurningPointCT.org: Online options and peer support by & for teens and young adults in CT. Statewide, based in Westport at Positive Directions.
  • NAMI: Support groups and training programs by & for families with a loved one with mental illness. 203-568-0826
  • The Hub: Regional resource lists for treatment and support. 203-579-2727
