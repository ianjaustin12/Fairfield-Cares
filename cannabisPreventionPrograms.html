Impact of THC on the Teen and Young Adult Brain

Fairfield CARES Community Coalition presented an educational community Webinar on Marijuana. Participants learned about current cannabis trends and paraphernalia, potential detrimental impact on youth, signs of youth use and what parents can do. Local experts in the counseling and therapy field participated and responded to questions relevant to our community.
