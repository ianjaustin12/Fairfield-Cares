The coalition's current substance misuse prevention goals and/or objectives include:

  1. Reduce the percentage of middle school and high school youth who drink alcohol in the past month to no more than 20%.
  2. 88% percent of middle and high school youth perceive underage drinking as harmful.
  3. Increase the percentage of youth who perceive vaping as harmful.
  4. Increase parent knowledge of the dangers of vaping.
  5. Begin work to advocate for banning the sale of flavored tobacco products locally in Fairfield.
  6. Increase the availability of opioid use disorder prevention materials throughout the community.
