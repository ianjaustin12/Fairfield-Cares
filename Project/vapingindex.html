Vaping/Nicotine
Prevention

Sadly, the proliferation of teen vaping has replaced the progress that was being made to reduce teen cigarette smoking. According to the 2021 National Youth Tobacco Survey (NYTS), 19.6% of high school students and 4.7% of middle school students currently use e-cigarettes, this is down from the previous year, but we also know that teens were not convening due to Covid. This is true in Fairfield as well. According to our 2021 student survey, 7% of high school students and 3% of middle school students vaped in the past 30 days, down considerably from 2019 and we suspect that the actual percentages are higher.
