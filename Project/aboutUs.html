About the Coalition

Mission Statement

The Fairfield CARES Community Coalition focuses on preventing substance misuse, fostering social and emotional wellness and resilience among youth, young adults, and families.

Our Mission

History

Fairfield CARES Task Force was established at the request of the First Selectman to address the problem of alcohol misuse particularly by youth and young adults.

Our History

Current Work

Fairfield CARES relies on the Town of Fairfield, the Fairfield School District, and non-profit organizations to serve as its fiscal agent for the grant work that it undertakes.
